onboarding checklist for new engineers — drafting this now because we've never onboarded anyone before (all 6 of us were founding team). feedback welcome:

- [ ] Participate in code review (both giving and receiving)
- [ ] On-call shadow for second day
- [ ] 30-day goals discussion with Priya

**Week 4: Full Contributor**
- [ ] Independent feature ownership
- [ ] Added to on-call rotation
- [ ] Retrospective: what worked, what was confusing, what needs improvement in onboarding

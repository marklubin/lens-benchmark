ARTICLE SUMMARY:

Rivalytics today announced a significant restructuring of their pricing and product strategy. Key changes:

1. PRICING: Standard tier drops from $49/mo per seat to $19/mo per seat, effective immediately. Existing customers will be migrated to the new pricing within 30 days. Annual billing drops to $15/mo per seat. The article notes that enterprise plans (100+ seats) will receive further volume discounts, though specific tiers were not disclosed. Free tier remains unchanged at 1 user with 1GB data cap.

2. NEW FEATURES: Rivalytics is launching "Rivalytics Live," a new real-time data layer that provides 15-minute data freshness (down from 6-12 hours). They describe this as "near-real-time analytics for the mid-market." Additionally, they are shipping an enhanced dashboard builder with "50+ new widgets, collaborative editing, and embeddable dashboard links." The article includes screenshots of the new dashboard builder showing drag-and-drop chart placement, a template gallery, and a share modal with public/private link settings.

3. POSITIONING: CEO Vanessa Cheng is quoted: "The mid-market analytics space has gotten noisier. We're making a statement: the best analytics product should also be the most accessible. $19/mo makes Rivalytics available to every growing company, not just those with big software budgets." Cheng also noted in a sidebar interview that Rivalytics's customer success team has grown from 12 to 31 people in the last quarter, citing "white-glove onboarding" as a differentiator against self-serve competitors.

4. FUNDING: Rivalytics has closed a $28M Series D round led by Sequoia. The round was announced alongside the pricing change. Additional participants include existing investors Benchmark and Y Combinator Continuity. Total funding to date is now $74M. The press release attached to the article indicates the round closed on May 22, 2025 — nine days before the public announcement — suggesting the pricing restructure was planned well in advance.

[09:43] Assistant: Building flavor is all about layering. Start by sautéing finely chopped aromatics—garlic, onion, maybe some fennel if you like—until they’re super fragrant. Then add a tablespoon of tomato paste and cook it for a couple of minutes until it darkens slightly—that step really adds richness. Next, add your canned tomatoes—whole peeled are great because you can crush them to control texture—and simmer for at least 30 minutes. For depth, splash in a bit of red wine or balsamic vinegar near the end. Finish with a pat of butter or a drizzle of good olive oil to add shine and richness. Season well with salt, pepper, and a pinch of sugar if needed.

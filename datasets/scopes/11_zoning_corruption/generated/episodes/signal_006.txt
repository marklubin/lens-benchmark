SUBJECT: Objection to Zoning Variance Request

TO: Zoning Board of Appeals
ATTN: Robert J. Miller, Chairperson

Dear Chairperson Miller and Members of the Board,

The Community Action Group submits this formal comment regarding the variance request filed by Lakeside Properties Group. The group has reviewed the documentation provided by the Applicant. The group has concerns regarding the impact on the surrounding residential area.

The group notes that the variance request seeks to reduce frontage requirements. The group believes this reduction may set a precedent for future developments. The group requests that the Board consider the cumulative impact of such reductions.

The group requests that the Board hold a public hearing on the matter. The group requests that the Board require additional environmental impact studies. The group requests that the Board require additional traffic studies.

The group believes that the current zoning code is sufficient to protect the interests of the community. The group believes that variances should be granted only in cases of extreme hardship. The group does not believe that extreme hardship exists in this case.

The group reserves the right to appeal any decision made by the Board. The group reserves the right to seek legal counsel. The group reserves the right to petition the City Council.

This comment is submitted for the public record. This comment is not an endorsement or opposition to the Applicant. This comment is an expression of concern regarding the process.

Sincerely,
Community Action Group
Board of Directors
June 14, 2024

End of Public Comment Submission.

RE: Zoning Verification - Parcel 14-05-22-101

This memo serves to confirm the zoning classification and land use compliance for the property located at 1242 Oak Street, Parcel ID 14-05-22-101. The subject property is currently zoned R-1, which designates Single Family Residential use.

The zoning classification R-1 permits the following uses:
1.  Single-family dwelling units.
2.  Home offices (subject to square footage restrictions).
3.  Accessory structures (garages, sheds) not exceeding 1,200 square feet.
4.  Public utilities and infrastructure necessary for residential service.

The property transfer recorded on June 7, 2024, does not require a zoning variance or special use permit. The lot size of 10,890 sq ft meets the minimum frontage requirements for the R-1 district. The acreage of 0.25 acres is consistent with the established density for this sector of the township.

A review of the Millbrook Zoning Code, Section 14.02, indicates that the transfer of ownership does not alter the permitted use of the land. The Grantee, Thompson Residential LLC, has agreed to abide by all covenants associated with the property, including the HOA Agreement.

The survey conducted by Millbrook Surveying Inc on May 15, 2024, confirms that the physical boundaries align with the recorded plat. There are no encroachments or boundary disputes noted in the survey report. The standard utility easement noted in the deed is consistent with existing infrastructure maps held by the Department of Public Works.

No additional zoning approval is required for this transfer. The property remains in compliance with the comprehensive plan for the year 2024.

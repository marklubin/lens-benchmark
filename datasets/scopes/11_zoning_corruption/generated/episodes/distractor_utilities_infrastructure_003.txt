CITY OF OAKHAVEN DEPARTMENT OF PLANNING
TECHNICAL REVIEW MEMORANDUM

FILE REFERENCE: UTIL-2024-003
DOCUMENT DATE: 2024-04-02
PREPARED BY: Sarah J. Chen, Senior Architectural Reviewer

SUBJECT: Architectural Plans Review for Substation B Upgrade

1.0 OVERVIEW

This memorandum summarizes the architectural review of the proposed modifications to Substation B, Parcel ID: EL-3310. The review was conducted in accordance with the City of Oakhaven Zoning Ordinance Chapter 14, Section 14.5 regarding Utility Infrastructure Facilities. The project involves the replacement of existing electrical equipment with modernized units to accommodate increased demand.

2.0 SITE LAYOUT AND SETBACKS

The proposed site layout maintains existing property lines. No new structures are being added outside the current perimeter fence. The new equipment footprint is identical to the previous equipment footprint.
Perimeter Fence: Existing 8-foot chain link fencing remains in place.
Access Road: Existing gravel access road at the rear of the property remains unchanged.
Setback Requirements: The project complies with the minimum 10-foot setback requirement for utility structures from adjacent residential properties.

3.0 STRUCTURAL SPECIFICATIONS

New transformers rated at 15 MVA to support growing commercial load.
Weight Distribution: The new units weigh approximately 12,000 pounds each.
Foundation: Concrete slab foundation with 4-inch thickness.
Anchor Bolts: High-tensile steel anchors securing equipment to foundation.
Seismic Rating: Equipment meets Seismic Zone 4 requirements as defined by the California Building Code (adopted by reference).

4.0 NOISE CONTROL MEASURES

Architectural plans show noise-reducing acoustic barriers around the new equipment.
Barrier Height: 12 feet total height.
Material Composition: Pre-cast concrete panels with internal acoustic foam lining.
Sound Attenuation Rating: NRC 0.85.
Expected Noise Level at Property Line: 45 dBA.
Background Noise Level at Property Line: 50 dBA.
Compliance: The design ensures sound levels do not exceed local noise ordinances.

5.0 MATERIALS AND FINISHES

Exterior Finish: Industrial grade epoxy paint, color RAL 7035 (Light Grey).
Roofing: Flat roof with metal flashing.
Drainage: Internal drainage system directs rainwater to existing storm sewer.
Fencing: Chain link with privacy slats installed on the north and west sides to screen equipment from view.

6.0 ELECTRICAL CONNECTIONS

High Voltage Connection: 138 kV transmission line.
Low Voltage Connection: 13.8 kV distribution line.
Grounding: 100% copper grounding grid installed.
Disconnect Switches: Manual disconnect switches installed at ground level for safety.

7.0 CONSTRUCTION ACCESS

Construction vehicles will enter from Powerline Road.
Designated Parking Area: South-west corner of the site.
Signage: Standard construction safety signage required.
Lighting: Temporary floodlights permitted for night work.

8.0 REVIEW COMMENTS

The review team notes the following items for contractor attention:
1. Ensure all temporary fencing is secured before work begins.
2. Verify grounding continuity prior to energization.
3. Submit final as-built drawings upon project completion.

This review does not constitute approval of the electrical work. Electrical permit required from Electrical Division.

1.0 INTRODUCTION

This Environmental Review Summary documents the findings of the environmental assessment conducted for the Substation B Upgrade. The review was performed in accordance with the City of Oakhaven Environmental Impact Policy (EIP) Section 8.1.

Electromagnetic field (EMF) analysis for Substation B was conducted by Third Party Environmental Consultants, LLC.
Methodology: Field measurements taken at 1-meter intervals around the perimeter.
Frequency Range: 50 Hz to 60 Hz.
Measurement Equipment: Triaxial Gaussmeter Model 3000.
Results: Projected fields at property line remain well below FCC safety limits.
Comparison:
- Measured Field Strength: 0.5 Gauss.
- FCC Safety Limit: 2.0 Gauss.
- Margin of Safety: 75%.

Soil contamination tests prior to excavation returned negative results.
Sample Points: 4 locations (North, South, East, West).
Depth: 0 to 6 feet.
Contaminants Tested: PCBs, Heavy Metals, Volatile Organic Compounds.
Results: All samples below detection limits.
Groundwater Depth: 15 feet below surface.
Aquifer Protection: No impact on drinking water sources expected.

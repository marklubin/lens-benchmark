The meeting was called to order at 6:00 PM by Chairperson Patterson. A quorum was present. The following members were in attendance: Chairperson Patterson, Member Kowalski, Member Rivera, Member Chen. Member Johnson was absent. The clerk recorded the roll call.

Staff Report No. 24-0712 was presented by Planning Director Smith. The report recommended approval of the special use permit for the development located at 1234 River Drive. The site plan was reviewed for compliance with zoning regulations. The environmental impact was reviewed. The traffic study was reviewed. Staff noted no objections to the proposed development.

PUBLIC HEARING
The public hearing was opened at 6:15 PM. The applicant was present. The applicant presented the project details. The applicant stated the project would provide additional housing units. The applicant stated the project would comply with all city codes. The applicant stated the project would not impact the surrounding properties.

PUBLIC COMMENT
The floor was opened for public comment. Three members of the public spoke. The first speaker supported the project. The second speaker opposed the project. The third speaker supported the project. The speakers were reminded to address the board. The speakers addressed the board. The speakers concluded their remarks. The public comment period closed at 6:45 PM.

MOTION AND VOTE
Chairperson Patterson moved to approve the special use permit for the development located at 1234 River Drive. Member Kowalski seconded the motion. The motion was put to a vote.

VOTE RECORD

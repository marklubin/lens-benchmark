NORTHWOOD INDEPENDENT SCHOOL DISTRICT
CONTRACT AGREEMENT
AGREEMENT NUMBER: NISD-2024-001
DATE OF EXECUTION: 2023-10-12

THIS AGREEMENT is made and entered into this 12th day of October, 2023, by and between NORTHWOOD INDEPENDENT SCHOOL DISTRICT, a political subdivision of the State of Texas (hereinafter referred to as the "District"), and the NORTHWOOD EDUCATION ASSOCIATION, a labor organization (hereinafter referred to as the "Association").

WHEREAS, the District desires to employ certified personnel to provide educational services to the students of the District; and

WHEREAS, the Association represents the certified employees of the District; and

WHEREAS, the parties desire to establish the terms and conditions of employment for the certified employees;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE I: DEFINITIONS

1.1 "Certified Employee" shall mean any employee of the District holding a valid teaching certificate issued by the Texas Education Agency.
1.2 "Fiscal Year" shall mean the period from September 1 of any year to August 31 of the following year.
1.3 "Academic Year" shall mean the period from the first day of instruction in September to the last day of instruction in June.
1.4 "Salary Schedule" shall mean the compensation scale attached hereto as Exhibit A.
1.5 "Benefits" shall mean the health, dental, and retirement benefits attached hereto as Exhibit B.

ARTICLE II: TERM

2.1 This Agreement shall become effective on July 1, 2024 (hereinafter referred to as the "Effective Date").
2.2 The term of this Agreement shall be for a period of 3 Years (hereinafter referred to as the "Contract Term").
2.3 The Contract Term shall expire on June 30, 2027.
2.4 Either party may provide written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the expiration of the Contract Term.
2.5 If no notice of non-renewal is provided, this Agreement shall automatically renew for an additional 1 Year period.

ARTICLE III: COMPENSATION

3.1 Base Salary.
3.1.1 The District shall pay the base salary of each Certified Employee according to the Salary Schedule set forth in Exhibit A.
3.1.2 The Salary Schedule shall reflect a salary increase of 4.5% for all steps on the schedule effective on the Effective Date.
3.1.3 The salary increase shall be prorated for any employee commencing employment after the Effective Date.
3.2 Longevity Pay.
3.2.1 Employees with five (5) or more years of service in the District shall receive a longevity pay increment of $500 per year.
3.2.2 Employees with ten (10) or more years of service in the District shall receive a longevity pay increment of $1,000 per year.
3.3 Stipends.
3.3.1 Stipends for coaching, advising, and department chairperson duties shall be paid in accordance with District Policy 3.10.
3.3.2 Stipends shall be paid on a monthly basis throughout the school year.

ARTICLE IV: WORKING CONDITIONS

4.1 Work Day.
4.1.1 The standard work day for Certified Employees shall be eight (8) hours.
4.1.2 The standard work week shall be five (5) days.
4.2 Class Size.
4.2.1 Class sizes shall not exceed thirty (30) students per classroom for elementary grades.
4.2.2 Class sizes shall not exceed thirty-five (35) students per classroom for secondary grades.
4.3 Planning Time.
4.3.1 Each Certified Employee shall receive at least one (1) planning period per day.
4.3.2 Planning periods shall not be assigned to non-instructional duties.

ARTICLE V: PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT

5.1 District Support.
5.1.1 The District shall allocate funds for professional development activities.
5.1.2 Funds shall be allocated based on the number of Certified Employees employed.
5.2 Leave for Professional Development.
5.2.1 Certified Employees may take up to five (5) days of professional development leave per year.
5.2.2 Professional development leave shall be with full compensation.
5.2.3 Professional development leave must be approved by the Superintendent.

ARTICLE VI: DISPUTE RESOLUTION

6.1 Grievance Procedure.
6.1.1 Grievances shall be processed in accordance with the District Grievance Procedure.
6.1.2 All grievances must be submitted in writing within ten (10) days of the incident.
6.2 Arbitration.
6.2.1 Disputes regarding the interpretation of this Agreement shall be submitted to arbitration.
6.2.2 The arbitrator shall be selected by mutual agreement of the parties.
6.2.3 The decision of the arbitrator shall be final and binding.

ARTICLE VII: TERMINATION

7.1 Termination for Cause.
7.1.1 The District may terminate employment for cause in accordance with state law.
7.1.2 Cause includes insubordination, negligence, or violation of District Policy.
7.2 Termination without Cause.
7.2.1 The District may terminate employment without cause upon payment of severance pay.
7.2.2 Severance pay shall be equal to one (1) month of salary for each year of service.
7.3 Resignation.
7.3.1 Certified Employees shall provide thirty (30) days written notice of resignation.
7.3.2 Failure to provide notice may result in forfeiture of unused sick leave.

ARTICLE VIII: GENERAL PROVISIONS

8.1 Governing Law.
8.1.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas.
8.2 Severability.
8.2.1 If any provision of this Agreement is held invalid, the remainder shall continue in full force.
8.3 Entire Agreement.
8.3.1 This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.
8.4 Amendments.
8.4.1 Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
8.5 Notices.
8.5.1 All notices shall be sent via certified mail to the addresses listed in this Agreement.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement on the date first written above.

CALL TO ORDER
The regular meeting of the Northwood Independent School District Board of Trustees was called to order by Board President Eleanor Vance at 6:00 p.m. The meeting was held in the Northwood High School Auditorium. A quorum was present.

ROLL CALL
Trustee Eleanor Vance (President) - Present
Trustee Marcus Thorne (Vice President) - Present
Trustee Sarah Jenkins - Present
Trustee David O’Connor - Present
Trustee Linda Martinez - Present
Trustee Robert Hayes - Present

Trustee Hayes arrived at 6:05 p.m. for the roll call. Trustee Vance noted the presence of the Superintendent, Dr. James Miller, and the Board Secretary, Mrs. Patricia Williams.

PLEDGE OF ALLEGIANCE
The meeting commenced with the Pledge of Allegiance to the Flag of the United States of America, led by Superintendent Miller.

APPROVAL OF AGENDA
Trustee Jenkins moved to approve the agenda as distributed. Trustee O’Connor seconded the motion. The motion carried unanimously.

CONSENT AGENDA
1. Approval of Minutes from November 7, 2023, Regular Meeting.
2. Approval of Financial Statements for October 2023.
3. Approval of Staff Appointments for the 2023-2024 Academic Year.

Trustee Thorne moved to approve the Consent Agenda as a block. Trustee Martinez seconded the motion. The motion carried unanimously.

SPECIAL PRESENTATIONS AND REPORTS
Superintendent’s Report
Superintendent James Miller provided a report on current district operations. He noted that enrollment remains stable at 14,500 students across 22 campuses. He highlighted the ongoing maintenance needs at Northwood High School. He stated that the HVAC systems at the main facility are approaching the end of their operational lifespan. He recommended the Board review the Facility Maintenance Proposal for the HVAC System Retrofit currently on the agenda. He noted that the project is essential for maintaining indoor air quality and temperature control during the winter months.

Treasurer’s Report
Treasurer Robert Hayes presented the financial report. He detailed the status of the Capital Improvement Bond. He stated that the bond issuance occurred in 2022. He noted that the current allocation for the HVAC System Retrofit is within the approved budget. He reported that the total estimated cost is three million four hundred thousand dollars. He confirmed that the funding source is the Capital Improvement Bond. He stated that the funds are available for disbursement upon Board approval.

FACILITY MAINTENANCE PROPOSAL (HVAC SYSTEM RETROFIT)
The Board reviewed the Facility Maintenance Proposal for the HVAC System Retrofit at Northwood High School. The proposal was presented by Facilities Director A. Brown. The project location is Northwood High School, located at 1200 Education Way. The estimated cost is $3,400,000. The funding source is the Capital Improvement Bond. The completion date is listed as 2024-06-30.

Trustee Jenkins asked for clarification on the timeline. Director Brown stated that construction would begin in January 2024. He noted that the work would be phased to minimize disruption to the academic calendar. He stated that the first phase would focus on the science wing. He noted that the final phase would be completed by June 30, 2024.

Trustee O’Connor asked about the funding breakdown. Director Brown provided a breakdown. He stated that equipment costs account for sixty percent of the budget. He stated that labor costs account for twenty-five percent of the budget. He stated that contingency funds account for fifteen percent of the budget.

MOTION TO APPROVE FACILITY MAINTENANCE PROPOSAL
Trustee Jenkins moved to approve the Facility Maintenance Proposal for the HVAC System Retrofit at Northwood High School. The motion specified the estimated cost of $3,400,000. The motion specified the funding source as the Capital Improvement Bond. The motion specified the completion date as 2024-06-30. Trustee Thorne seconded the motion.

A roll call vote was taken on the motion.
Trustee Vance: Aye
Trustee Thorne: Aye
Trustee Jenkins: Aye
Trustee O’Connor: Aye
Trustee Martinez: Aye
Trustee Hayes: Aye

The motion carried.

INTER-DEPARTMENTAL MEMO (SAFETY COMPLIANCE)
The Board reviewed the Inter-Departmental Memo regarding Safety Compliance. The document was submitted by Facilities Director A. Brown. The section is labeled Safety Compliance. The status is Approved. The reviewer is Facilities Director A. Brown.

Trustee Martinez asked if the memo addressed the safety protocols for the construction workers. Director Brown stated that the memo outlines the safety protocols. He noted that all workers must wear personal protective equipment. He stated that the site must be secured during non-working hours. He noted that the Environmental Review Summary was attached to the memo.

Trustee Hayes asked about the timeline for the safety review. Director Brown stated that the review was completed on November 30, 2023. He noted that the approval was granted on December 1, 2023. He stated that the memo is now part of the official record.

MOTION TO APPROVE INTER-DEPARTMENTAL MEMO
Trustee O’Connor moved to approve the Inter-Departmental Memo regarding Safety Compliance. The motion specified the status as Approved. The motion specified the reviewer as Facilities Director A. Brown. Trustee Jenkins seconded the motion.

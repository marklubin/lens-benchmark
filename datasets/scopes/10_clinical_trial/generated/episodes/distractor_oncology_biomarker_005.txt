STUDY OBJECTIVES
PRIMARY OBJECTIVE: TO EVALUATE THE EFFICACY OF THE STUDY DRUG IN PATIENTS WITH METASTATIC NON-SMALL CELL LUNG CANCER
SECONDARY OBJECTIVE: TO EVALUATE THE SAFETY AND TOLERABILITY OF THE STUDY DRUG
EXPLORATORY OBJECTIVE: TO EVALUATE PHARMACOKINETIC PARAMETERS AND BIOMARKER CORRELATIONS
BIOMARKER ANALYSIS: PD-L1 EXPRESSION AND CIRCULATING TUMOR DNA QUANTIFICATION

INCLUSION AND EXCLUSION CRITERIA SUMMARY
INCLUSION CRITERIA:
1. HISTOLOGICALLY CONFIRMED NON-SMALL CELL LUNG CANCER
2. METASTATIC OR UNRESECTABLE DISEASE
3. PD-L1 TUMOR PROPORTIONAL SCORE GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 1%
4. ECOG PERFORMANCE STATUS 0 OR 1
5. LIFE EXPECTANCY GREATER THAN OR EQUAL TO 12 WEEKS
6. ADEQUATE ORGAN FUNCTION
7. AGREEMENT TO USE CONTRACEPTION

EXCLUSION CRITERIA:
1. PREVIOUS THERAPY WITH THE STUDY DRUG
2. ACTIVE AUTOIMMUNE DISEASE
3. HISTORY OF ORGAN TRANSPLANTATION
4. UNCONTROLLED HYPERTENSION
5. SERIOUS CARDIOVASCULAR DISEASE
6. PREGNANCY OR BREASTFEEDING
7. OTHER MALIGNANCY WITHIN 5 YEARS
8. HISTORY OF SEVERE ADVERSE REACTION TO IMMUNOTHERAPY
9. INABILITY TO SWALLOW ORALLY ADMINISTERED MEDICATIONS
10. CONCURRENT PARTICIPATION IN OTHER INTERVENTIONAL STUDY

COMPLIANCE CALCULATION METHODOLOGY
COMPLIANCE IS DETERMINED BY THE FOLLOWING FORMULA:
COMPLIANCE = (ACTUAL DOSES ADMINISTERED / EXPECTED DOSES) * 100
EXPECTED DOSES ARE BASED ON THE PROTOCOL SCHEDULE.
DELAYED DOSES ARE COUNTED AS ADMINISTERED IF WITHIN 14 DAYS OF SCHEDULE.
DOSES WITHHELD FOR MEDICAL REASONS ARE EXCLUDED FROM THE DENOMINATOR.
DOSES DISCONTINUED EARLY ARE EXCLUDED FROM THE DENOMINATOR.
PATIENTS WITH COMPLIANCE LESS THAN 80% ARE FLAGGED FOR REVIEW.
PATIENTS WITH COMPLIANCE GREATER THAN 120% ARE FLAGGED FOR REVIEW.
NON-COMPLIANT PATIENTS MAY BE SUBJECT TO DISCONTINUATION.
COMPLIANCE DATA IS REPORTED MONTHLY TO THE DATA SAFETY MONITORING BOARD.

PHARMACOKINETIC SUMMARY SECTION

PHARMACOKINETIC STUDY DESIGN
SERUM SAMPLES ARE COLLECTED AT SPECIFIED TIMEPOINTS.
TIMEPOINTS INCLUDE PRE-DOSE, POST-DOSE, AND ELIMINATION PHASES.
SAMPLES ARE STABILIZED AND STORED AT -80 DEGREES CELSIUS.
ANALYSIS IS PERFORMED USING VALIDATED LIQUID CHROMATOGRAPHY MASS SPECTROMETRY.
CALIBRATION CURVES ARE GENERATED FOR EACH BATCH ANALYSIS.
QUALITY CONTROL SAMPLES ARE INCLUDED IN EACH RUN.
LIMIT OF QUANTIFICATION IS 0.5 NG/ML.
LIMIT OF DETECTION IS 0.1 NG/ML.
DATA IS PROCESSED USING NON-COMPARTMENTAL ANALYSIS SOFTWARE.

PHARMACOKINETIC PARAMETERS DEFINITIONS
CMAX IS THE PEAK SERUM CONCENTRATION.
TMAX IS THE TIME TO REACH PEAK SERUM CONCENTRATION.
HALF-LIFE IS THE TIME REQUIRED FOR CONCENTRATION TO REDUCE BY HALF.
AREA UNDER THE CURVE IS THE TOTAL EXPOSURE OVER TIME.
CLEARANCE IS THE VOLUME OF PLASMA CLEARED OF DRUG PER UNIT TIME.
VOLUME OF DISTRIBUTION IS THE THEORETICAL VOLUME REQUIRED TO CONTAIN THE DRUG.

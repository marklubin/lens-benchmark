STUDY OBJECTIVE: Evaluate efficacy and safety of XR-7491 in moderate-to-severe rheumatoid arthritis.
PRIMARY ENDPOINT: ACR20 response rate at Week 12.
SECONDARY ENDPOINTS: ACR50, ACR70, DAS28-CRP, HAQ-DI, remission rates.
STUDY DESIGN: Randomized, Double-Blind, Placebo-Controlled, Parallel-Group, Multicenter.
TOTAL PLANNED ENROLLMENT: 240 subjects.
RANDOMIZATION RATIO: 3:3:3:1 (5mg, 15mg, 30mg BID, Placebo).
NUMBER OF DOSING ARMS: 4
ARM 1: XR-7491 5mg Oral Tablet, BID.
ARM 2: XR-7491 15mg Oral Tablet, BID.
ARM 3: XR-7491 30mg Oral Tablet, BID.
ARM 4: Matching Placebo Oral Tablet, BID.
STUDY SITES: 12 clinical centers across North America.
SITE-001 STATUS: Open and Active.
INCLUSION CRITERIA:
1. Male or female, age 18 to 75 years.
2. Diagnosis of Rheumatoid Arthritis meeting 2010 ACR/EULAR Classification Criteria.
3. Disease duration >6 months.
4. Active disease defined as ≥6 swollen joints and ≥6 tender joints at screening.
5. Inadequate response to at least one Disease-Modifying Antirheumatic Drug (DMARD).
6. Stable dose of background methotrexate for ≥8 weeks prior to screening.
7. Signed Informed Consent Form (ICF).
EXCLUSION CRITERIA:
1. Active hepatitis B or C infection.
2. ALT >1.5x Upper Limit of Normal (ULN).
3. AST >2x ULN.
4. History of malignancy within 5 years (excluding non-melanoma skin cancer).
5. Active tuberculosis or history of latent TB without treatment.
6. Pregnancy or breastfeeding.
7. Concomitant use of biologics or JAK inhibitors within 4 weeks.
BASELINE VISIT SCHEDULE: Scheduled for all randomized subjects within 24 hours of randomization.
BASELINE ASSESSMENTS: Physical exam, vital signs, ECG, laboratory panel (CBC, CMP, LFTs, lipids), pregnancy test, RA disease activity scores (DAS28, ACR core set), concomitant medication review.
STUDY DURATION: 12 weeks treatment, 4 weeks follow-up.
TOTAL STUDY DURATION: 16 weeks.
SCHEDULE OF EVENTS:
Visit 1 (Screening): Informed consent, eligibility check, ICF signature, baseline labs.
Visit 2 (Baseline/Day 1): Randomization, drug dispensing, first dose administration, safety assessment.
Visit 3 (Week 2): Safety, tolerability, compliance check.

STUDY OVERVIEW AND PROTOCOL SUMMARY
This document constitutes the monitoring snapshot for the ongoing Phase IIb clinical investigation designated ONC-204. The study is conducted under Good Clinical Practice (GCP) guidelines and the Declaration of Helsinki. The therapeutic indication for this investigational product is Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer (NSCLC). The study design is randomized, open-label, multicenter. The primary objective involves the evaluation of safety and tolerability of the investigational agent administered intravenously. Secondary objectives include assessment of pharmacokinetic profiles and preliminary efficacy signals within specific biomarker-defined subgroups.

Inclusion Criteria Summary
1. Histologically or cytologically confirmed diagnosis of Stage IV Non-Small Cell Lung Cancer.
2. Performance status ECOG 0-1.
3. Life expectancy of at least 12 weeks.
4. Measurable disease per RECIST 1.1 criteria.
5. Adequate organ function as defined in the protocol.
6. Willingness to provide informed consent.

Exclusion Criteria Summary
1. Prior treatment with the investigational agent class.
2. Active autoimmune disease requiring systemic therapy.
3. History of interstitial lung disease.
4. Pregnancy or breastfeeding.
5. Concomitant use of CYP3A4 inhibitors or inducers.
6. Uncontrolled infection.

STUDY OVERVIEW
The Phase IIb clinical trial for XR-7491, an oral Janus Kinase (JAK) inhibitor, is currently in the baseline data collection and initial enrollment phase. The study is designed to evaluate the efficacy and safety of three distinct dosing levels (5mg, 15mg, and 30mg) of XR-7491 administered twice daily (BID) compared to placebo in adult subjects with moderate to severe rheumatoid arthritis (RA) who have had an inadequate response to at least one Disease-Modifying Antirheumatic Drug (DMARD).
